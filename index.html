How it works

A simple check before you hit send

  1. Paste in the message you want to send.
  2. See whether it sounds helpful, pushy, guilt-inducing, or overwhelming.
  3. Use the rewrite to make it shorter, softer, and easier to receive.

Why it helps

Good intentions do not always sound good on a screen.

Many grandparents are trying to show love, concern, and experience. GrandpAI helps that care come through without sounding critical, intrusive, or hurtful.

It does not tell you to say more. It helps you say less, more kindly, and only when it is useful.
